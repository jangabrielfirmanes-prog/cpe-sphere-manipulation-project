How to Use the Demo

  1. Click "Launch Demo" to begin the demonstration
  2. Allow camera access when prompted by your browser
  3. Use your left hand to control the sphere:
    • Pinch thumb and index finger together to resize the sphere
    • Touch the center of the screen with your middle finger to change colors
  4. Use your right hand position to rotate the sphere in 3D space
  5. Click "Connect ESP32" to integrate with external hardware

Tip: Ensure your hands are clearly visible to the camera with adequate lighting for optimal tracking.
